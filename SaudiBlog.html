Saudi Arabia

Saudi Arabia, the birthplace of Islam and home to its two holiest cities—Mecca and Medina—stands as a nation where deep-rooted traditions harmoniously coexist with forward-looking ambitions. Under the leadership of King Salman and Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman, the Kingdom has embarked on a transformative journey through its Vision 2030 initiative, aiming to diversify its oil-dependent economy and position itself as a global hub for innovation and culture .
